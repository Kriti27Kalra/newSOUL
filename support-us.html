Education belongs to everyone. Let's make it a reality.

In Noida, India, many migrant families struggle for survival, living in slums with limited resources. Their children, often overlooked, face a bleak future without education. SOUL Satya Hari School, run by the NGO SOUL, is their beacon of hope.

Our Impacts

From humble beginnings with just a few children learning basic skills, our journey has grown into a powerful movement. Today, 60 children receive not only quality education in essential subjects but also lifeenriching activities like sewing and cooking, nurturing both knowledge and selfreliance. Through the EWS scheme, we bridge the gap for graduating students to transition into formal schoolsachieving remarkable milestones with 3 success stories in 2015, 4 in 2016, 7 in 2017, and 8 in 2018. Today, SOUL Satya Hari School stands as a benchmark for child education, recognized for its unwavering commitment to empowering underprivileged children.

Why We Educate

At SOUL NGO, we believe education is the most powerful tool for change. It empowers children to face challenges, dream bigger, and break free from the cycle of poverty. Educated children grow into contributing members of society, uplifting their families and communities. We especially prioritize girls education, challenging societal norms and paving the way for a more equitable and hopeful future.

    Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)


    What is the impact of my donation on the education of undeserved children ?

    Your donation has a direct and significant impact on the education of underprivileged children. It helps provide access to quality education, learning resources, and support services. Your contribution enables these children to break the cycle of poverty, gain knowledge and skills, and have a brighter future.

    How will SOUL keep the update on progress of the children supported through my donation?

    SOUL values transparency and keeps donors updated on the progress of the children supported through regular communication. You will receive newsletters, impact reports, or personalized updates on the children's achievements, academic progress, and success stories.

    Are my donations eligible for tax benefits?

    Yes, donations made are eligible for tax benefits under the applicable provisions of the income tax laws in your country. You will receive a tax receipt for your donation, which you can use for tax purposes.

    How does SOUL ensure transparency and accountability in the utilization of funds for the programmes?

    SOUL maintains strict financial guidelines and undergoes regular audits to ensure transparency and accountability in the utilization of funds. We publish financial reports on our website, providing a breakdown of the expenses and impact created by the programmes.

    Is there a minimum or maximum donation amount to support the SOUL initiatives?

    SOUL appreciates donations of all sizes. There is generally no minimum or maximum donation amount to support the ME programme. You can contribute according to your capacity and willingness to support the cause.
